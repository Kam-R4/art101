How to make tea

  • Take out a tea bag, cup, coffee stick, and kettle.
  • Fill the kettle with water
  • Turn on kettle until water is boiling
  • Place tea bag into the cup
  • Pour the boiling water into the cup
  • Wait 3-5 minutes
  • Add sugar or honey or lemon after the 3-5 minutes if you want
  • Stir the tea in a circle motion with a coffee stick.
  • Enjoy the tea!
